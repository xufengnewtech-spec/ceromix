About CEROMIX

A global technology leader

CEROMIX Group is an international technology group and a leading supplier of high-tech materials such as carbon, technical ceramics, and sintered metals — as well as machines and systems for the world-leaders in industry and technology.

Founded in 1913, the company has grown from a small specialized manufacturer into a global player with over 10,000 employees across 64 locations in 26 countries. Our engineering competence spans the entire value chain — from material development and component manufacturing to complete system solutions.
